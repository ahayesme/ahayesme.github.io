Vegan Holiday Cookie Boxes

VEGAN SNICKERDOODLE

Yield: 20 cookies

Time: 25 minutes

INGREDIENTS

FOR THE COOKIE:

  • ½ cup vegan butter (112 grams)
  • ⅔ cup white granulated sugar (132 grams)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour (188 grams)
  • 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspooon baking soda
  • 1 ¼ teaspoon cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup vegan chocolate chips

PREPARATION

STEP 1

Add the butter and sugar to an electric mixer and cream them together. Add in the vanilla and apple cider vinegar and mix in.

STEP 2

Add in the flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, cinnamon and salt and mix in by hand (don’t use the electric mixer for this part) until it forms a crumbly dough.

STEP 3

Then add in the soy milk so that it forms into a proper cookie dough (as pictured).

STEP 4

Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

STEP 5

Roll the cookie dough into balls and then roll the balls in the cinnamon and sugar mixture and place them onto a parchment lined baking tray. You can sprinkle more of the cinnamon sugar mixture on top of the balls before they go in the oven.

STEP 6

Bake for 10 minutes.

STEP 7

Remove from the oven and let the cookies firm up and cool down directly on the tray.

FUDGY VEGAN BROWNIES

Yield: 9 brownies

Time: 30 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • ½ cup melted vegan butter - hot
  • 1 ½ cups granulated cane sugar
  • ⅓ cup non-dairy milk (we used oat milk)
  • 3 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • ¾ teaspoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon soy milk or other non-dairy milk (we used oat milk)

FOR ROLLING:

  • 3 tablespoons white granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • a dash of nutmeg

PREPARATION

STEP 1

Preheat the oven to 350°F.

STEP 2

First, line an 8x8 baking pan with parchment paper. Refer to the photos above or the notes below for the best way to do this. Then rub some softened butter on top of the parchment paper.

STEP 3

In a large bowl, whisk together the hot melted butter and sugar. Then, whisk in the milk and vanilla.

STEP 4

In a separate bowl, combine the dry ingredients: flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.

STEP 5

Mix the dry ingredients into the bowl with the butter and sugar mixture until well combined.

STEP 6

Fold in the chocolate chips.

STEP 7

Spread the batter evenly into the baking pan.

STEP 8

Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a fork comes out relatively clean. (Keep in mind that if you hit a chocolate chip, the fork won't be clean - just make sure there is no wet brownie batter on it. Some crumbs are okay.)

STEP 9

Let cool for about 30 minutes in the pan. Then grab the parchment paper handles and pull the brownies out. Set on a wire rack to finish cooling completely before cutting.

STEP 10

For clean-cut brownies, run a clean knife under hot water in between each slice.

STEP 11

Store leftover brownies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days.
